Investi

Investi is an autonomous AI financial analyst and trader living on Telegram.

It orchestrates a swarm of specialized agents to analyze technicals, fundamentals, and news in real-time. Simply chat with it to screen stocks, manage your portfolio, and execute trades.

This is an experiment and probably a terrible idea. We strongly suggest not putting any real money into this. Consider it more of a "what if" than actual financial advice. However, if you feel crazy and confident enough, the code is yours to tinker with!
